Privacy Policy

For Luna at OZ Tattoo · Hollywood Blvd, Los Angeles, CA

This page is a general template and does not constitute legal advice. You should have a licensed attorney review and customize this policy to make sure it fits your specific business and location.

1. Information we collect

When you contact or book with Luna, we may collect:

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, and phone number
  • Booking details such as preferred appointment dates, tattoo ideas, and placement
  • Any reference images or descriptions you choose to share
  • Communication preferences, including your choice to opt in or opt out of text messages

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to booking requests and questions
  • Schedule, confirm, or reschedule appointments
  • Send important updates related to your booking or aftercare
  • Send optional studio updates or promotions if you have opted in to receive them
  • Maintain internal records and protect the safety and integrity of the studio

3. Text messages and communication consent

On the booking form, you will see a clear option to opt in or out of receiving text messages. If you choose to opt in, you agree that Luna and OZ Tattoo may contact you by text at the phone number you provide.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency may vary. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text you receive, or by contacting the studio and asking to be removed from text communication.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. Your information may be shared only as reasonably necessary, for example:

  • With OZ Tattoo studio management for booking and studio operations
  • With service providers that help manage booking forms, messaging, or email (for example, scheduling or CRM tools)
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of clients and staff

5. Data retention

We may retain your information as long as reasonably necessary to manage bookings, maintain records, and comply with legal or regulatory requirements. If you would like your information updated or deleted, you can contact the studio using the details below.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Please do not send highly sensitive personal information through the booking form or standard text messages.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights, such as the ability to:

  • Access or request a copy of your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request that your information be deleted, subject to any legal obligations
  • Change your communication preferences or withdraw consent to receive text messages

8. Updates to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request changes to your information, please contact Luna at OZ Tattoo. You can also reach out using the contact form on the main website.
